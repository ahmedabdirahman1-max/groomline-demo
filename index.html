Questions groomers ask

Does it sound like a robot?

No — a natural, friendly voice that greets callers by your business name and handles real back-and-forth. Try the live demo and judge for yourself.

Do I need MoeGo or another booking app too?

No. The calendar, deposits, reminders and rebooking are built in — GroomLine is the whole front desk, not a layer on top of other software.

Do I have to change my number?

Never. We forward only the calls you'd otherwise miss, and reply to texts on your line. Your number, your phone, unchanged.

What about a badly matted or aggressive dog?

Welfare first — it flags those cases for you to review personally before any booking, so you can plan the safe approach.
